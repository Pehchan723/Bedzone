Our Story

Founded in 2010, PureSalt Industries began as a small family business with a vision to provide the highest quality salt products to Pakistan and beyond. Today, we stand as the country's leading salt manufacturer, serving diverse industries from food processing to pharmaceuticals.

Our commitment to excellence, sustainable practices, and customer satisfaction has earned us ISO certifications and the trust of over 1000 satisfied clients worldwide.

1970 - Company Founded

Started as a small salt processing unit in Rawalpindi

1985 - First Export

Began exporting to Middle East markets

2000 - ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 quality certification

2020 - Modern Facility

Opened state-of-the-art manufacturing plant
